Zug Zug or Ishnu'alah? Welcome to Call to Arms, a listing for World of Warcraft Classic. World of Warcraft Classic, often abbreviated as WoW Classic, is a version of the popular MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game) World of Warcraft (WoW) developed by Blizzard Entertainment. WoW Classic is designed to replicate the original experience of WoW as it was at the time of its initial release in 2004, prior to any of the expansions and major updates that have significantly changed the game over the years.
